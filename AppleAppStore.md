## Description (4000 chars max)

Rizedown is a focused writing and meeting capture tool for Mac. Record your meetings, get automatic transcriptions, and turn everything into clean, structured Markdown notes — all without leaving your keyboard.

**Record and transcribe meetings**
Capture microphone and system audio simultaneously. Rizedown records your voice alongside meeting output from Teams, Zoom, or any other app, then transcribes everything on-device using Apple Speech or Whisper.net — no cloud, no data leaving your Mac.

**Write in Markdown**
A distraction-free editor built for Markdown. Type in the syntax editor or switch to the visual preview at any time. Keyboard shortcuts keep your hands on the keyboard.

**Organize by workspace**
Open any folder as a workspace. Your notes live as plain `.mds` files on disk — no proprietary formats, no lock-in. Browse, rename, and delete files directly from the sidebar.

**Export to PDF**
Export any document to a clean, typeset PDF with a single command. Tables, code blocks, headers, and inline formatting all render correctly.

**Private by design**
Transcription runs entirely on-device. Rizedown never sends your audio, notes, or recordings to any server.

## Reviewer Notes (for App Review)
Rizedown records microphone and system audio (via ScreenCaptureKit) for the purpose of transcribing meetings. All transcription is performed on-device using Apple Speech framework or Whisper.net (CoreML). No audio or transcript data is transmitted to any server.

To test audio recording: click the record button in the toolbar. The app will request microphone and Screen Recording permissions on first use.
